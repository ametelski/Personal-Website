About Me

United States Navy
Honor • Courage • Commitment

I joined the Navy in January 2003 and after boot camp and training I was station on the USS Abraham Lincoln in Everett Washington. In my first two years I was an Airman (“a green shirt”), working on the flight deck launching and recovering aircraft. In my next two years I decided to change my position and become a Engineman Maintenance Technician. As an Engineman I was responsible for the operation and maintenance to four emergence diesel generates that could power the entire ship if was required.

The Navy provided me many opportunities and experiences that has shaped me into the person that I am now, and I am very grateful that could serve my country.
